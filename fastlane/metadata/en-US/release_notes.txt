New in 2016.0 and 2016.1:
* Anime Detour 2016 schedule, guests, and maps
* Schedule and guest information bundled in the app - no internet service required for most of the app
* Use the `Now` button to jump to ongoing events during Anime Detour
* Notifications for favorite events, 10 minutes before they start
* Colors in the schedule for each type of event
* 3D Touch peek and pop support in the main schedule and guest list
* Full multitasking support on devices that support it
* Picking a day goes to 9AM
* Events with an age requirement now include a note about requiring an ID
* Guests are shown under the correct headers
