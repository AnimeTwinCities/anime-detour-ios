The official iOS app of Anime Detour for 2016. View the convention schedule, hotel maps, and more.

- See the full list of events, or only one category at a time. Looking for photo shoots? Pick it in the Filter view.
- The full schedule now supports favorites! Mark events to save for later, and turn on notifications for when they're starting soon. Turn notifications on – or off – in the Info tab or the Settings app.
- Search sessions by name.
- See the guests coming to Anime Detour this year.
- Use the hotel map to find rooms by name.
- Tap the hotel name in the Info tab to get directions in Maps.
