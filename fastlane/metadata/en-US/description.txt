The official iOS app of Anime Detour for 2016. View the convention schedule, hotel maps, and more.

- See the full list of sessions, or only the category you want. Looking for photo shoots? Pick it in the Filter view.
- The full schedule now supports favorites! Mark sessions to save for later, and turn on notifications for when they're starting soon. Turn notifications on – or off – in the Info tab.
- Search sessions by name.
- See the guests coming to Anime Detour this year.
- Use the hotel map to find the room for a session.
- Tap the hotel name in the Info tab to get directions in Maps.
