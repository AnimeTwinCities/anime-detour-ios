The official iOS app of Anime Detour for 2017. View the convention schedule, hotel maps, and more.

- See the full list of events. See what's going on right now using the Now button.
- Star events to save them to your schedule. Turn on notifications to get alerts when they're starting soon. Turn notifications on – or off – in the Info tab or the Settings app.
- See the guests coming to Anime Detour this year.
- See a map of the Dealers' Room, the whole hotel, and nearby locations of interest.
- Tap the hotel name in the Info tab to get directions in Maps.
